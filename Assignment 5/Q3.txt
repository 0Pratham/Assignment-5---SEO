Q -> Meta tags .
Ans -> Meta tags are pieces of information we use to tell the search engine and those viewing our site more about our page
	and the information it contains . It include title tags , meta description , etc.
	
	Benefits -> 
	1 : Its title tag and meta description length , will impact how relevant our site appears in SERF(Search engine result pages) . when users 
	    receive their list of results , they will see our site title and short description - the meta descriptiion.

	2 : Improved search engine optimization(SEO) - Meta tags can help improving the viusibility and ranking of our website in search engine
	    results by providing information about the content of our webpage to search engine.

	3 : Better user experience by socia media sharing .

	4 : Characters encoding - ensures that browser interprets the text , characters correctly .
