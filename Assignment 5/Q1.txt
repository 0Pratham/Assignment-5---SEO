Ans -> SEO stands for Search engine optimization .
	It is the process of improving our website to increase its visibility in google , microsoft bing , and many other 
	search engine whenever people search for products we sell , and services we provide.
	It is the process used to optimize a website's technical configuration , content relevance and link popularity 
	so its pages can become more relevant and popular towards user search queries , and as a consequence , search engine 
	rank them better . 
	It is important because to help attract website visitors who will become customers , client or an audience that keeps coming back .
