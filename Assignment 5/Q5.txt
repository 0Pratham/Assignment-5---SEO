Q -> Favicon.
Ans -> An 16*16 oixel icon associated with particular website , typically displayed in the address bar of a browser 
	accessing the site.It is hsort for "favorite icon" .They are also found on our browsers's bookmark bar , history 
	and in search results , alongside the page url(uniform resource locator) .

	<head>
	    <link rel="icon" href="icon URL" type="image/icon"
	</head>
	--> the image present on the URL will be added as favicon.
